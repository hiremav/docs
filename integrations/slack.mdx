Mav's Slack integration lets you automatically push lead updates, outcomes, and events from Mav directly into your Slack workspace. Once connected, you choose exactly which activities trigger a notification and which channel they're delivered to — keeping your team informed without leaving Slack.

## Setting Up the Integration

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Slack configuration">
    In Mav, navigate to **Integrations** and click **Configure** on the Slack integration.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect your Slack workspace">
    Click **Connect your Slack Workspace**. You'll be redirected to Slack's authorization page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Grant access">
    Review the requested permissions and click **Allow** to let Mav access your Slack workspace.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select activities and a channel">
    Choose which events and outcomes you want Mav to send, and select the Slack channel where notifications should appear.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Save**. Lead activity updates will begin appearing in your selected channel immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  You need to be a Slack workspace admin, or have permission to install apps, before completing the authorization step.
</Note>
