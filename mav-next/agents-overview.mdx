An **agent** is the AI that works your leads. It holds the conversation, asks the right qualifying questions, answers the contact, and knows when to bring in a human. Each agent is purpose-built for an insurance vertical and works leads across both SMS and live phone calls.

## What an agent does

Every agent comes with three things working together:

- **A persona** — the name and voice it uses, so it sounds like your brand.
- **Instructions** — how it should behave, what to prioritize, and how to handle objections.
- **Qualifying questions** — the details it gathers (for example coverage type, vehicles, address) to qualify the lead.

The agent pursues these conversationally rather than as a rigid script — it adapts to what the contact says, in the contact's own language.

## Works across SMS and voice

The same agent runs the text conversation **and** the phone call. It can text a lead, place a call, transfer to a rep, and follow up by text afterward — all as one continuous relationship, with full memory of everything said on either channel. See [Conversations & Channels](/mav-next/voice) for how this works.

## Always compliant

Customer-facing agents have TCPA safeguards built in — they honor STOP and HELP keywords, include the required messaging disclosures, and respect your texting and calling hours. See [Compliance](/mav-next/tcpa-and-consent) for details.
