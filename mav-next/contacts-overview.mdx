The **Contacts** page is your window into every person Mav.next is working. From here you can browse all your contacts, open any one of them, and follow the full conversation in real time.

## The contacts list

The list shows everyone in your account with at-a-glance status. Use the [filters](/mav-next/filtering-contacts) to narrow it down — by conversation status, call outcome, direction, date, and more — then click any contact to open their detail view.

## The contact detail view

Opening a contact shows everything in one place:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Profile" icon="id-card">
    Name, phone, email, time zone, and language. You can edit core fields inline.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Conversation feed" icon="comments">
    The complete history — every SMS in and out, plus voice-call transcripts — in one chronological thread.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Collected details" icon="clipboard-list">
    Everything the agent has qualified, such as vehicles, address, or coverage details.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Call recordings" icon="phone">
    Playable recordings of any calls on the conversation. See [Call recordings](/mav-next/call-recordings).
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Note>
  Because SMS and voice live in one thread, the feed reads as a single conversation even when the contact moved from texting to a phone call and back.
</Note>

## What you can do from here

- Read and audit the full conversation, including what the agent collected.
- Listen to [call recordings](/mav-next/call-recordings).
- [Mark a conversion](/mav-next/converting-a-contact) when a contact quotes or buys.
- [Export](/mav-next/exporting-contacts) a filtered set of contacts for reporting.
