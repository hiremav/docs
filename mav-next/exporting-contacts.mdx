When you need your data outside Mav — for reporting, a CRM sync, or compliance records — export your contacts to a CSV.

## Exporting a list

<Steps>
  <Step title="Filter to the contacts you want">
    On the [Contacts](/mav-next/contacts-overview) page, use the [filters](/mav-next/filtering-contacts) to build the exact segment — for example, all converted contacts from a given date range.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start the export">
    Choose **Export**. Mav generates a CSV of the filtered set in the background, so you can keep working.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get your file">
    When the export is ready, it's delivered to you and available to download from **Settings → Exports**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What's included

The export reflects the contacts and the filters you selected — their profile details and the outcomes Mav has recorded — so the file matches what you saw on screen.

<Tip>
  Exports honor your filters exactly. If your numbers look off, re-check the active [filter chips](/mav-next/filtering-contacts) before exporting.
</Tip>

<Note>
  Past exports are listed under **Settings → Exports**, where you can download them again.
</Note>
