Mav.next is the next generation of the Mav platform. Instead of separate tools for texting and calling, a single AI **agent** works each lead across **SMS and voice in one continuous conversation** — reaching out, qualifying, answering questions, and connecting the lead to a rep when they're ready. This page explains what Mav.next does, how a lead moves through it, and where to go next.

<Note>
  Mav.next is the new Mav experience, rolling out alongside the classic platform. If you're looking for docs on classic Playbooks and the standalone Party Line, see the [Documentation](/introduction) section.
</Note>

## What Mav.next does

Insurance leads go cold in minutes. Mav.next responds the instant a lead arrives and keeps working it until there's an outcome — with no manual dialing and no handoff gaps between channels.

The difference from classic Mav is that one agent owns the **whole relationship with a contact**. It can text a new lead, qualify them over SMS, place a call when the moment is right, transfer them live to a rep, and pick the text conversation back up afterward — all as a single thread of history the agent remembers. Your team spends its time on high-intent, already-qualified conversations.

## How Mav.next works

<Steps>
  <Step title="A lead arrives and an agent picks it up">
    Leads enter Mav.next from a **marketing source**, a **CSV import**, or your own integration. Each lead becomes a **contact**, and the agent you've assigned begins an engagement — sending the first text within your compliance hours.
  </Step>
  <Step title="The agent qualifies over SMS">
    The agent holds a natural, two-way conversation: gathering qualifying details, answering questions, and re-engaging the contact if they go quiet. Everything it learns is saved to the contact and surfaced in your reporting.
  </Step>
  <Step title="It moves to a live call when the lead is ready">
    When a contact wants to talk — or simply texts “call me” — the agent can place a phone call and transfer the lead live to an available rep, then bridge the two together. The same agent runs the call, so nothing is repeated or lost.
  </Step>
  <Step title="The conversation continues and converts">
    After a call, the agent seamlessly returns to SMS to follow up, and records the outcome — qualified, quoted, sold, or not interested — so your CRM and reporting stay in sync.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## TCPA compliance

<Warning>
  You are responsible for ensuring every lead you send to Mav.next has given consent to be contacted via automated SMS and voice calls, as required by the Telephone Consumer Protection Act (TCPA). Mav.next honors STOP and HELP keywords automatically and can scrub incoming leads against suppression lists before an agent ever reaches out.
</Warning>
