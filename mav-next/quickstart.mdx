## Get your first lead flowing

<Steps>
  <Step title="Review your agent">
    Open **Agents** and select the agent set up for your account (for example, Auto Insurance). Check its [persona and instructions](/mav-next/configuring-an-agent) so it sounds like your brand.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send a test conversation">
    Use [**Send a test**](/mav-next/testing-an-agent) to text yourself — or trigger a test call — and experience the agent exactly as a lead would. Iterate on the persona until it feels right.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect a lead source">
    Set up a [marketing source](/mav-next/marketing-sources) so your vendor or web form can deliver leads straight to the agent, or [import a CSV](/mav-next/importing-contacts) to load a batch yourself.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set your availability">
    Configure [transfer hours](/mav-next/account-and-transfer-hours) so live calls are only attempted when your reps can actually take them.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Watch it work">
    As leads arrive, open **Contacts** to follow each [conversation](/mav-next/contacts-overview) in real time and review outcomes.
  </Step>
</Steps>
