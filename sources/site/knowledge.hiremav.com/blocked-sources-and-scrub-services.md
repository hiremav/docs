# Blocked Sources and Scrub Services

#### **What are Blocked Sources and Scrub Services?**

**Blocked Sources** lets you prevent certain lead sources (e.g. sub-affiliates, publishers, or vendors) from sending leads into your Mav account. If a source continues to submit leads, Mav will automatically reject them and you can track rejection reasons.

**Scrub Services** enables you to automatically check incoming leads against external suppression lists and block submissions that match. Currently Mav supports scrubbing against the **Blacklist Alliance** and the [**DNC.com**](https://dnc.com/) database.

These two features work together to give you more control over lead quality and compliance.

---

#### **How it works**

### **Blocked Sources**

**![53ded78b](https://knowledge.hiremav.com/hs-fs/hubfs/53ded78b.png?width=670&height=250&name=53ded78b.png)**

1. Navigate to **Settings →Filters** in your Mav dashboard
 
2. Click **Blocked Sources**
 
3. Enter the Source(for example, an affiliate ID, campaign name, or domain) and the Sub-Source if applicable
 
4. Click **Save**
 

### **Scrub Services**

**![8df16bf1](https://knowledge.hiremav.com/hs-fs/hubfs/8df16bf1.png?width=670&height=291&name=8df16bf1.png)**

1. Go to **Settings →Filters**
 
2. Click **Scrub Services**
 
3. Toggle on one or both of the scrubbing databases you wish to use
 
 - Blacklist Alliance
 
 - DNC.com
 
4. Enter your API key when requested
 
5. Click **Save**
 

---

#### **Use cases & best practices**

- **Reject known low-quality sources**: If a vendor continuously sends invalid or fraudulent leads, block them to prevent further damage to your conversion metrics.
 
- **Ensure compliance with Do-Not-Call rules**: With Scrub Services, you reduce legal and regulatory risk when contacting leads by phone.
 
- **Maintain list hygiene**: Scrubbing protects you from wasting resources on leads that are already suppressed due to prior complaints or blacklist registration.
