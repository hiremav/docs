# Setting up a one-click Slack integration 

## Send Mav outcomes and events to your Slack workspace using our one-click integration. 

### **Overview**

**Mav's Slack integration allows you to automatically push updates outcomes and events from Mav directly into your Slack Workspace. Once connected, you can select which events and outcomes you’d like to be notified about.** 

### **Setting up the integration**

1. In Mav, navigate to Integrations
 
2. Click Configure 
 
 ![Screenshot 2026-04-17 at 12.50.08 PM](https://knowledge.hiremav.com/hs-fs/hubfs/Screenshot%202026-04-17%20at%2012.50.08%20PM.png?width=670&height=450&name=Screenshot%202026-04-17%20at%2012.50.08%20PM.png)
 
3. Connect your Slack Workspace 
4. Allow Mav to access your Slack Workspace 
5. Select the activities that you want to send to your Slack Workspace and the channel where you want them directed
6. Once connected, event updates about your leads will appear in the channel.
